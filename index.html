HER PURGATORY

Game DesignLevel Design

Her Purgatory is a 3D top down, four player drop in dungeon exploration game. The story surrounds a young girl trapped in an endless nightmare cycle about her toys who hurt her. You play as her Brother's action figures to fight and free her torment.

There are four classes in the game each with their own attack style and abilities that the player has the option to upgrade and change in the Village Hub. Throughout the dungeons there are many combat and puzzle encounters and two bosses.

Her Purgatory was developed in 3.5 weeks during a GameJam by a team of five people.

Oliver Cullen, Joshua Bromley, Pavan Mankoo, Deniss Kaminskis, Ayrton Milner and Arthur Fostier.
