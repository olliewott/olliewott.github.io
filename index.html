IMPOSSIBLE RESCUE

Game DesignLevel DesignScriptingCombat DesignNarrative Design

Impossible Rescue is an action-packed twin-stick shooter with an infusion of original music and Roman-inspired sci-fi visuals.

In a desperate race against time, assume the role of an undercover mercenary, Danielle, to infiltrate and retrieve vital stolen data from the Empire on Mars against an ever-imposing resistance.

What I did on this project:

  • Level Design for two out of four levels.
  • Gameplay Design and Narrative
  • Created and implemented a series of mechanics using Blueprints in Unreal Engine.

DESCENDANTS OF ARTIO

Game DesignLevel DesignNarrative DesignPuzzle Design

Descendants of Artio is a 3D adventure game inspired by celtic mythology.

The balance of an ancient world is disrupted. Explore forgotten temples, traverse the land and use unique abilities. Take the role of a demigod as you set upon a mission to restore the equilibrium.

Descendants of Artio was developed as part of my Final Major Project for my undergrad degree in Birmingham City University. I was in a team that consisted of three artists, three coders, two designers and one producer.

On this project I did the following:

  • Complete Narrative Backstory including world and characters to aid concept artists.
  • Level Design and Flow for the whole playable area.
  • Light Puzzle Design and Balance
  • Storyboard and Cutscene Design

HER PURGATORY

Game DesignLevel DesignNarrative DesignCombat DesignProduction

Her Purgatory is 3D top-down, four-player drop in dungeon exploring game in which a young girl is trapped in a cycle of nightmares in which her toys her hurt. You play as one of her older brother's action figures as you battle to free her from her torment.

In Her Purgatory there are four different classes that have been created that have their own list of skills and abilities that the player can level up and unlock in the Village Hub by killing enemies and collecting their souls. The dungeons in the game have a series of challenges including combat, puzzle and also bosses.

The game was created in 3.5 weeks by a team of 6 University students as part of a Game Jam. The team was: Oliver Cullen, Joshua Bromley, Pavan Mankoo, Deniss Kaminskis, Ayrton Milner and Arthur Fostier.

On this project I did the following:

  • Narrative Synposis and World Design.
  • Developed and balanced a series of class abilities for the players to unlock.
  • Designed and Balanced the Final Boss "The Doll" as seen in the trailer.
  • Supported the Art Team by creating smaller environment assets.
